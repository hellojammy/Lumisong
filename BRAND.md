# Lumisong Brand Identity

## Canonical Name

- Primary English name: `Lumisong`
- Secondary Chinese name: `鸣光`
- Chinese name must be visually weaker than `Lumisong` and should not appear inside the app icon.

## Product Meaning

Lumisong is a 3D audio visualization tool. It turns music, voice, birdsong, and ambient sound into a luminous spatial map of sound events. It is not a bird app and not a generic music player.

## Logo Concept

Use the **glowing sound-event atlas**:

- 5 primary glowing sound-event nodes.
- 12 to 18 weaker secondary event dots.
- 1 cyan playback light path moving through the primary nodes.
- 1 restrained orange accent node for the current energy/playback focus.
- A dense but low-contrast perspective grid below the events.

## Visual Rules

- Main background: `#05070d`
- Main accent: ice blue / cyan, close to `#67e8f9`
- Secondary blue: `#38bdf8` / `#60a5fa`
- Energy accent: `#f97316`, only one small accent
- Grid should remain subtle and never compete with the nodes.

## Do Not Use

- Birds
- Musical notes
- Microphones
- Vinyl records
- Headphones
- Waveform bars
- Staff notation
- Literal galaxy spirals
